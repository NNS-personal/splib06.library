SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Hematite spectral absorption features are seen with an intense ultraviolet-centered absorption from 0.3 to 0.55 microns, a feature at 0.66, and a strong feature at 0.88 microns. A hint of an OH absorption feature (clay or mica) is seen at 2.2 microns. This spectrum is identified as being from coarse grained hematite.
