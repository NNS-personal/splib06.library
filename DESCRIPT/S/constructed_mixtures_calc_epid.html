SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Spectra have absorptions characteristic of epidote at 0.4154, 0.4575, 0.4811, 1.0544, and 1.5421 microns. The large absorption at 2.338 microns is mostly due to epidote with some contribution from a superimposed calcite absorption.
