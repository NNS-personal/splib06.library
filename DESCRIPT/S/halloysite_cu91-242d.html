SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

This sample is from the southwestern portion of the western alteration center at Cuprite, NV. The sample is from altered lower Cambrian Harkless Phyllite hydrothermally altered about 7.5 Ma.

END_SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION.

XRD_ANALYSIS:

Based on glycolation halloysite was found. Also present are quartz, mica, alunite, and possibly dickite.

END_XRD_ANALYSIS.

SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

The spectrum has absorptions characteristic of halloysite with a band at 2.2 microns with a weak shorter wavelength shoulder. The presence of trace amounts of alunite are indicated by the weak band at 1.477 microns.

See: Swayze, G.A., 1997, The hydrothermal and structural history of the Cuprite Mining District, southwestern Nevada: an integrated geological and geophysical approach: unpublished Ph.D. Dissertation, University of Colorado at Boulder, 399p.

END_SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION.
