SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Averaged spectral reflectance for a conifer forest dominated by whitebark pine located on the slopes of Electric Peak (in the Gallatin Mountain Range) in Yellowstone National Park. Average computed from 105 pixels of radiative-transfer-ground-calibrated AVIRIS data.  AVIRIS data were collected on August 6, 1996, at approximately 11:10 am Local Time.  AVIRIS channels 1-4, 32-33, 43, 59-62, 81-84, 95-97, 106-113, 154-167, 173-175, and 222-224 were deleted due to residual atmospheric effects, poor instrument response, and redundancy where detectors overlap.  Sample referenced in Kokaly and others (2002).

Kokaly and others (2002), "Remote Sensing of environment 5792 (2002)1-20"
