SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Grass from side of road just west of Bld. 20. Antigorite sample is pale green massive antigorite with picrolitic partings also made of antigorite. X-ray diffraction indicates that the sample is composed of antigorite (a non-fibrous serpentine mineral in the same mineral group as chrysotile asbestos).
