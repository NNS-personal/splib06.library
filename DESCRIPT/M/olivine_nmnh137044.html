SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

The sample consists of many 0.5cm particles plus one large fragment. Under petrographic microscope, the hand-picked sample appears clean but one of the grains may be pyroxene.

Salisbury, J. W., Walter, L. W., and Vergo, N., 1987, Mid-Infrared (2.1-25µm) Spectra of Minerals: First Edition, U.S. Geological Survey Open File Report 87-263.
