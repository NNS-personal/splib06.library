XRD_ANALYSIS:

Corrensite + albite + quartz, albite ~8 vol. %, quartz ~4%, corrensite coats albite and quartz grains.

Clark, R.N., T.V.V. King, M. Klejwa, G. Swayze, and N. Vergo, 1990, High spectral resolution reflectance spectroscopy of minerals: J. Geophys Res. 12653-12680.

END_XRD_ANALYSIS.

COMPOSITION_DISCUSSION:

Corrensite is a regular 1:1 interstratification of trioctahedral chlorite and trioctahedral smectite or vermiculite.

Clark, R.N., T.V.V. King, M. Klejwa, G. Swayze, and N. Vergo, 1990, High spectral resolution reflectance spectroscopy of minerals: J. Geophys Res. 12653-12680.

END_COMPOSITION_DISCUSSION.
