SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Sample has relatively strong vibrational features between 1.4 - 1.5, 1.76, 2.17, 2.32, and 2.5 microns. Other pure Na-endmember alunites prepared at lower temperatures have much weaker vibrational features.
