COMPOSITION_DISCUSSION:

Microprobe analysis showed the sample to be homogeneous within and between grains. Average of seven analyses does not include lithium, boron or water, but demonstrates the presence of sufficient manganese to indicate that this is not end member elbaite.

Salisbury, J. W., Walter, L. W., and Vergo, N., 1987, Mid-Infrared (2.1-25µm) Spectra of Minerals: First Edition, U.S. Geological Survey Open File Report 87-263.
