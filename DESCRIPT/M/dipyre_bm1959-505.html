SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Scapolites are metamorphic minerals with compositions suggestive of the feldspars. Dipyre is an intermediate composition of the solid solution series marialite-wernerite-meionite.

Two spectra are in the library, one of the original sample, and one after HL separation.
