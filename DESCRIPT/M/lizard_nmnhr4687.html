SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Forms series with Neupouite. Polymorphous with Antigorite, Clinochrysotile, Orthochrysotile, and Parachrysotile.

The sample was ground in an alumina mortar and pestle and wet sieved using methanol into <30µm (d), 60-104µm (c), 150-250µm (b) and >250µm (a) size fractions. The letter denotes spectrum designation.
