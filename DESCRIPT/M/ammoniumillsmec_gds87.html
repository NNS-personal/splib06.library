XRD_ANALYSIS:

40 kV - 30 mA, 6.5-9.5 keV
File: amilsm87_mdi (smear on quartz plate)
References: Deer, Howie and Zussman (1962); Srodon and Eberl (1984); PDF2 #29-1496
Found: Rectorite and/or illite
Sought: illite, mascagnite

Comment: The pattern consists of broad peaks, some having shoulders or tails extending toward lower d-spacings. The alpha1-alpha2 reflections are not resolved. Both rectorite (29-1495, a 1:1 regularly interstratified dioctahedral mica and dioctahedral smectite) and 1M illite (29-1496) explain most features of the pattern. Neither has a basal reflection exactly in the observed position, 11.0 Angstroms. Weak but sharp and symmetric reflections at 6.10 and 5.45 Angstroms are not identified; in a subsequent bulk mount, the intensity at 5.45 Angstroms was greatly enhanced and that at 6.10 Angstroms greatly reduced. Glycolation of the bulk mount so reduced all intensities that the pattern was useless.

END_XRD_ANALYSIS.
