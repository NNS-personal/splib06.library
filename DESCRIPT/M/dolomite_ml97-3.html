SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Spectrum has very strong saturated doublet at 0.9858 and 1.3450 microns probably related to Fe in the dolomite. Strong absorption at 2.3191 microns is due to C-O related and slightly shorter than wavelengths of non-ferroan dolomite (e.g. 2.321 microns).
