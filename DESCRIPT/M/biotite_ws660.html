XRD_ANALYSIS:

Trioctahedral mica - major - probably a mixture of compositions. No other components.

Konnert, Judith and Marta Flohr, 1992, unpublished data, USGS Reston, VA.

40 kV - 30 mA, 7.3-9.5 keV
File: annit660.out, -.mdi (edge=random mount)
References: Huebner's reference patterns; JCPDS #42-1413, 33-1016
Found: trioctahedral mica, 3T or 1M polytype

Comment: Basal reflections are surprisingly weak, even considering the use of a random mount. Some peak broadening or splitting suggests compositional heterogeneity of zoning. The position of the (060) is consistent with Fe/(Mg+Fe+Mn) = 0.25 +/- 0.05 (phlogopite), but not annite or siderophyllite.

J.S. Huebner, J. Pickrell, T. Schaefer, written communication(USGS)
