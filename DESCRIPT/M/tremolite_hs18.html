SAMPLE_DESCRIPTION:

Forms series with Actinolite and Ferroactinolite.

"S-1A. Amphibole, variety Tremolite. St. Lawrence Co., N.Y. (18B). This sample has a fairly prominent broad band near 1µ, indicating that it contains some ferrous ion. It displays a very sharp band at 1.4µ, and less sharp bands between 2.0 and 2.5 µ due to the overtone and combination tones of the OH stretch, respectively. In particular, the bands at 2.2 and 2.3 µ are due to combination tones of the OH stretch with lattice modes. The weak band at 1.9µ is probably due to a small amount of molecular H2O in the sample."

Hunt, G.R., J.W. Salisbury, 1970, Visible and near-infrared spectra of minerals and rocks: I. Silicate minerals. Modern Geology, v. 1, p. 283-300.
