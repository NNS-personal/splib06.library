SPECTROSCOPIC_DISCUSSION:

Spectrum begins at 1.4 microns and slopes downward toward longer wavelengths. There is a broad symmetrical absorption centered at 2.049 microns and three narrow absorptions at 2.3460, 2.3573, and 2.3634 microns.
